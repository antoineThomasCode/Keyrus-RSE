Keyrus CSR
Strategy

In 2011, Keyrus signed up to the United Nations Global Compact. Gradually, we have put in place governance to anchor ourselves in sustainable development: creation of the Keyrus Foundation in 2017, implementation of the CSR management in 2020 to steer a CSR strategy on our 3 ESG pillars.​

Environmental

GHG management, IT equipment life cycle, responsible digital and waste/recycling management.​

Social

Employee training, health and quality of life at work, ensuring gender equality and guaranteeing diversity and inclusion.​

Governance

Customer satisfaction, cybersecurity, ethics and compliance, and including CSR in all levels of business​
